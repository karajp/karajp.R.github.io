Randal Pound is a Melbourne based long term painter. He has returned to painting after a career in product design and has experimented with various art styles and mediums in recent years. These mixed artistry skills have developed his Expressionism style. Randal's artworks are abstract but recognisable images conveying people, fields of figures, still life of things left behind and landscapes. He aims to convey emotional response and the impact that people have on the world around them.

Randal has a Bachelor of Arts from Victorian College of the Arts as well as a Masters in Art from Monash University.

  • Art Studies: 1973 – 1975 RMIT [part-time]
  • 1976 – 1979 BA (Painting) VCA
  • 1993 – 1994 MA (Craft) Monash University

Randal has artwork at the National Gallery of Victoria a part of the Michell Collection. Over the years he has also accumulated awards and had his artwork displayed in many exhibitions.

  • 2019 Banyule Works on Paper Finalist Exhibition
  • 2016 ‘Diversarty’ Exhibition Eltham Library Foyer Gallery
  • 1995 Graduate Exhibition Cambridge Street Gallery
  • 1983 Southwark Gallery London UK
  • 1980 Graduate Exhibition Grant Street Gallery VCA
  • The Trustees of the National Gallery of Victoria Special Award 1979
  • The Fleay Bursary 1979
  • The Hugh Ramsay Portrait Prize 1978
  • Initial Year Painting Prize VCA 1977
